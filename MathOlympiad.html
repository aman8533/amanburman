UAE Mathematics Olympiad

I was selected to the UAE Mathematics Olympiad Squad after multiple rounds of country level mathematics competitions in 2021/2022 to prepare for the IMO 2023, participated in the intensive Olympiad camps held in the Spring and Summer of 2022 and solved various Olympiad level challenging problems under the supervision of former Olympiad gold medalist: Alexander Gavrilyuk.
Topics covered: Number Theory: GCD, LCM, Euclid division algorithm, modulo arithmetic, Fermat’s theorem, Diophantine equations, Algebra: Polynomials, quadratic equations, maxima, minima, graphical method of solving equations, Combinatorics: Permutation and combination, recurrence, sets, graph theory, Inequality: Cauchy Schwartz inequality, holder’s inequality, RM-AM-GM-HM inequality, Geometry: Ptolemy theorem, Ceva’s theorem, Menelaus theorem etc.
